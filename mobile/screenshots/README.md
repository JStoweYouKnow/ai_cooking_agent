# App Store Screenshots

Organize your screenshots here by device size.

## Folder Structure

- **6.7-inch/** - iPhone 15 Pro Max, 14 Pro Max (1290 x 2796 pixels)
- **6.5-inch/** - iPhone 11 Pro Max, XS Max (1242 x 2688 pixels)
- **5.5-inch/** - iPhone 8 Plus (1242 x 2208 pixels)

## Naming Convention

Name your screenshots with numbers to indicate order:
- `01-dashboard.png`
- `02-recipe-detail.png`
- `03-ai-assistant.png`
- `04-shopping-list.png`
- `05-pantry.png`

## Requirements

- **Minimum:** 3 screenshots per device size
- **Recommended:** 5-10 screenshots per device size
- **Format:** PNG
- **Content:** Must show actual app content (no mockups)

## Upload to App Store Connect

When ready, upload these screenshots to App Store Connect:
1. Go to App Store Connect → Your App → App Store tab
2. Scroll to "Screenshots and App Preview"
3. Upload screenshots for each device size
