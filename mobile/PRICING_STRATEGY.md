## ğŸ Trial Strategy

### Free Trial Options

**Option 1: 14-Day Free Trial (Recommended)**
- No credit card required
- Full Premium access for 14 days
- Convert to Free tier after trial
- Conversion funnel in app

**Option 2: 7-Day Free Trial**
- Credit card required upfront
- Auto-convert to paid after 7 days
- Higher conversion rate but lower signups

**Option 3: Feature-Based Trial**
- Unlock Premium features one-by-one
- "Try meal planning for free this week"
- Gamified progression

**Recommendation:** Start with **14-day no credit card** to maximize signups, then A/B test.
